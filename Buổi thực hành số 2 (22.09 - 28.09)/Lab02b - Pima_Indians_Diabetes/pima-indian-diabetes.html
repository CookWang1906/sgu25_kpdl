Phn loi bnh tiu ng trn b d liu Pima Indians Diabetes

1. nh ngha vn (Define Problem)

  • M t:
    • B d liu Pima Indians Diabetes l mt b d liu phn loi c s dng d on xem mt bnh nhn n ngi Pima Indian, t nht 21 tui, c b tiu ng hay khng da trn cc ch s chn on nht nh.
    • y l mt bi ton phn loi nh phn (Binary Classification).
  • D liu vo (8 thuc tnh):
    1. Pregnancies: S ln mang thai.
    2. Glucose: Nng Glucose trong huyt tng sau 2 gi lm xt nghim dung np Glucose ng ung (OGTT).
    3. BloodPressure: Huyt p tm trng (mm Hg).
    4. SkinThickness: dy np gp da c tam u (mm).
    5. Insulin: Insulin huyt thanh 2 gi (mu U/ml).
    6. BMI: Ch s khi c th (kg/(m^2)).
    7. DiabetesPedigreeFunction: Chc nng ph h tiu ng (nh gi nguy c di truyn).
    8. Age: Tui (nm).
  • Kt qu (Output): Outcome (Bin lp, 0: Khng tiu ng, 1: Tiu ng).
